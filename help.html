Help

This page provides help and support for imalogit.com.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost or price?
The site is free. It is paid for by the ads on the page.
Do you look at my logs and entries?
No. We only check size to prevent misuse. Your content should be simple text and numbers, not binary or binary-to-text encoding like Base64 and such.
Can I see the code behind the site?
Sure. It's at https://github.com/GeorgeHernandez/imalogit. The content of this site is free and open-source software (FOSS) or free/libre and open-source software (FLOSS), licensed under an ISC License.
